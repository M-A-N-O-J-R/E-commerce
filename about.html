JORDINDIAN

ABOUT

Jordindian is an Indian sketch comedy and music YouTube channel created by Naser Azzeh and Vineeth ′Beep′ Kumar. Their music video ′Smoke Shisha Play FIFA′ fetched them fame. They have created many videos that frequently appear on the list of trending videos in India. This duo has appeared in many Youtube fanfests and is popular throughout the country. Naser is from Jordan whereas Vineeth is from India. On 27 May 2019, they uploaded their second music video called 'Fasting And Furious' and it got 400,000 views within 3 hours and their 'Types of Doctors' video earned 1, 000,000 views in the first day. Their sketches are mostly centred around South Indian life and their style is a fusion of modern Indian, Jord, and Western cultures. They released their third song known as 'Exam Time' in April 2020 and also released another song during the COVID-19 pandemic lockdown in India known as 'Locked up in the Lockdown'.


Naser-Al-Azzeh is a Jordanian dancer who is the founder of the dance group Black Ice Crew. Vineeth "Beep" Kumar is a beat-boxer who has interviewed Salman Khan, A.R. Rahman, Shahrukh Khan, Mika, the cast of The Avengers etc. In a recent interview with Jaby Koay, Naser and Vineeth said that they met while in college in 2007. Their love of Russell Peters made them friends, especially when they realised that they actually lived in the same neighbourhood. They were initially going to make 3 videos and share them to others on social media and the target was 10,000 views. However, the 3 videos had such a good following, they decided to start a Youtube channel, and for a while they didn't put anything on it. They reached the trending list in India with their parody of the Fast and Furious 8 trailer. When Naser and Vineeth realized that the fast and furious video had hit 1 million views, they decided that they would do the channel full time. So they quit their jobs and began making videos. When Naser initially floated the idea of Jordindian a few years ago, Vineeth thought it wouldn’t work. "Clearly, I was wrong", Vineeth said in a 2019 interview. They were featured in YouTube Rewind 2018.
